THE GRATEFUL DEAD

"There is a road, no simple highway, between the dawn and the dark of night." - Jerry Garcia

Some background on The Grateful Dead:

  • Formed in 1965 under the name "the Warlocks".
  • First show as "The Grateful Dead" was December 4, 1965.
  • Garcia came up with the band name by turning to a random page in the dictionary.
  • Band performed (minus Pigpen after his death in 1973) until 1995, when Jerry Garcia passed.
  • Final show performed as The Grateful Dead was July 5, 2015 at Soldier Field in Chicago, Illinois.


To learn more about The Grateful Dead, be sure to check their official site and Wikipedia pages.
